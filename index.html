Services

From referral to reimbursement,
routed without the friction.

Waypoint Health places patient referrals with the nation's top surgeons, coordinates the delivery of care, and ensures every party is reimbursed quickly and accurately.

WAYPOINT 01

Referral Placement

We place patient referrals with highly credentialed, experienced surgeons across bariatric, general, spine, ortho, and other specialties — in every major and most minor U.S. market. Non-network providers are engaged via single-case agreements as needed.

WAYPOINT 02

Care Coordination

Our care coordination team ensures referred patients are promptly contacted and scheduled, working closely with providers, practice staff, and referral partners to guarantee an exceptional experience for everyone.

WAYPOINT 03

Claims & Billing

Our in-house billing team files timely, accurate invoices with payer and referral partners, and guarantees reimbursement to clinical partners — most often through a simple, efficient bundled model.
